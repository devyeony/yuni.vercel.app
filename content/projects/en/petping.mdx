## 📌 Project Name
- **Petping**

## 📋 Project Summary
- **Petping** is an **all-in-one healthcare app** that enables **integrated management** of **walks**, **nutrition**, **shopping**, and **insurance** for **pets**.
- It offers **diverse features** that promote a **healthy lifestyle** for **pets** and their owners, while continually enhancing the **user experience** and **operational efficiency**.

## 📅 Duration
- July 2021 – January 2024

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Backend:** Java, Spring, Spring Boot, JPA, MyBatis
- **Frontend:** JavaScript, HTML, CSS
- **Database:** MySQL, PostgreSQL, Redis
- **DevOps:** Docker, Jenkins, Linux
- **Cloud Infrastructure (AWS Services):** EC2, S3, ECS, ElastiCache, CodeDeploy, IAM, Route 53
- **Monitoring & Logging:** Grafana, Loki
- **Data Visualisation & BI Tools:** Redash

## 👤 Role & Contributions
- **Led backend architecture and operations:** Oversaw the entire backend lifecycle, including architecture design, development, deployment, and maintenance. Proactively contributed to infrastructure management and process improvements.
- **Cross-functional team player:** Collaborated closely with PMs, designers, frontend/app developers, and QA engineers, ensuring smooth communication and active involvement in feature development.
- **Data-driven development advocate:** Worked closely with marketers and data engineers to lead data structure design and collection workflows, maintaining a strong commitment to data-informed service enhancement.
- **Customer-centric problem solver with strong accountability:** Utilised server logs and data to identify customer issues and requirements, proposing solutions and guidance to support the CS team’s effective response. Facilitated smooth coordination with relevant technical teams for thorough issue resolution.
- **Proactive contributor beyond core responsibilities:** Engaged broadly across the service, offering ideas, detailed planning feedback, and overseeing testing and post-deployment monitoring. Continuously tracked customer feedback and data to detect issues early and led collaborative improvements.



## ✨ Key Features
| **Feature** | **Description** |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Pet Care Dashboard** | Summary of pet health data, weather and air quality (PM) information |
| **Pet Profile** | Set up pet profile, assign mission pet, share family code |
| **Walking Features** | Record urination, defecation and marking, audio-guided walks, save walking routes, find walking buddies nearby |
| **Nutrition Features** | Search pet food, log meals, analyse calorie intake, manage recurring diets, track allergies |
| **Health Calendar** | Integrated walk and diet calendar, health diary, health metric logging, social sharing of health records |
| **Missions & Rewards** | Missions for check-ins, walks and events; earn, spend and expire points |
| **Push & Notification Centre** | Automatic push alerts based on user activity, mission progress notifications, in-app alerts |
| **Commerce Features** | Linked pet shop with point usage, recommended and personalised product banners |
| **Pet Insurance** | Manage pet insurance contracts, renewals and claims; linked with insurers; integrated pet insurance rewards |
