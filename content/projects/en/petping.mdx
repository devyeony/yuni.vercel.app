## âœ¨ Key Features
| **Feature** | **Description** |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Pet Care Dashboard** | Summary of pet health data, weather and air quality (PM) information |
| **Pet Profile** | Set up pet profile, assign mission pet, share family code |
| **Walking Features** | Record urination, defecation and marking, audio-guided walks, save walking routes, find walking buddies nearby |
| **Nutrition Features** | Search pet food, log meals, analyse calorie intake, manage recurring diets, track allergies |
| **Health Calendar** | Integrated walk and diet calendar, health diary, health metric logging, social sharing of health records |
| **Missions & Rewards** | Missions for check-ins, walks and events; earn, spend and expire points |
| **Push & Notification Centre** | Automatic push alerts based on user activity, mission progress notifications, in-app alerts |
| **Commerce Features** | Linked pet shop with point usage, recommended and personalised product banners |
| **Pet Insurance** | Manage pet insurance contracts, renewals and claims; linked with insurers; integrated pet insurance rewards |
